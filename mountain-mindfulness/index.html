    Questions
    Frequently Asked
    Do I need yoga experience?

    Not at all. This retreat is designed for all levels — from complete beginners to experienced practitioners. Dom meets you exactly where you are.

    What's the cancellation policy?

    Full refund if you cancel 14 or more days before the retreat start date. Within 14 days, your spot can be transferred to someone else.

    What should I bring?

    Comfortable clothes for yoga, hiking shoes, a journal, sunscreen, and an open mind. Yoga mats and props are provided. Big Bear can be cool in the mornings — bring layers.

    Where is Sessions Retreat & Hotel?

    41421 Big Bear Blvd, Big Bear Lake, CA 92315. About 2 hours from Los Angeles. We're nestled in the San Bernardino Mountains at 6,750 feet elevation.

    Is the cold plunge required?

    Nothing is mandatory. Every activity is an invitation, not a requirement. You'll be encouraged to explore your edges, but always at your own pace.

    What if I'm coming alone?

    Most people come solo. The small group format (only 15 people) means you'll quickly feel connected. Many past participants say the people they meet become lifelong friends.
